Drug Advisory Training Hub

Syed Zulfiqar Hussain

Syed Zulfiqar Hussain is a renowned Consultant, Anti Drugs/Narcotics Campaign, Master Trainer on Drugs Use Prevention (UNODC, INL & DP, COLOMBO PLAN) and working in the field of drug demand reduction, best campaigner, counselor coach for substance use disorders (SUDs), tobacco control program like litigation, policy, implementation & sensitization and also best humanity service providers, etc. Syed Zulfiqar has done a lot in the field of drug demand reduction, tobacco control in educational institutions and public places. He served on the social side and changed the better life of under privilege people in society. He also started a new campaign against ‘New Innovative Tobacco related Products in Pakistan”. He got so much training on national and international levels regarding drug demand reduction, youth development, and tobacco control. He also represents two organizations Youth Council for Anti-Narcotics (YOCFAN) and Drug Advisory Training Hub (DATH).

Objective of the Organization

  • 01The main theme being Prepare today’s youth for tomorrow responsibilities.
  • 02To stress the role of students/youth in drug abuse prevention.
  • 03To provide the accurate information about drugs in the society.
  • 04To identify the problems relating drugs in the society.
  • 05To provide free basic health facilities for drug addicts.

Vision / Mission

Vision

Vision of YOCFAN is to make an ideal world where all people can spend life with pleasure particularly children, women and young people may empower with all kinds of desire to take healthy decisions avoiding all kinds of drug.

Mission

Drug Advisory Training Hub (DATH) & (YOCFAN) have been working in multiple directions like policy-making, planning to prepare the draft for legislation to forbid drug addiction through Sheesha Cafes and also Oxi Shots etc. DATH/YOCFAN also organized multiple activities like seminars, school, college talk programs, conferences, walks, rallies, study tours, youth exchanges programs, free treatment program and counseling for substance use disorder, distribution of food & water to homeless SUDs, monthly data collection unknown dead bodies homeless SUDs and to build better image and coordination with other government departments regarding drug demand reduction activities.
